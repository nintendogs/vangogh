death of van gogh

During his life, Van Gogh suffered poverty and mental illness. A well known instance is when he cut off his ear while chasing someone with a razor blade, then gifting the severed ear to a woman at a brothel. The townsfolk of his village agreed to sign him into a mental asylum. A year after he got out, under the watch of a doctor, he was found in a field with a gunshot wound in his chest, assumed to be self-inflicted. Two days later, with Theo by his side, he passed away - the date being July 29, 1890.
